Richard Li

Co-Founder


Richard is currently a grade 11 AP student at Markville Secondary School. With a vision to ensure that no one gets left out of society due to mental health, Glimmers of Hope was born. Outside of Glimmers of Hope, Richard is passionate about business, transportation planning, and software engineering, and he likes to advocate for better public transportation in cities.
